Oré mi means “my friend” in Yorùbá

The friend to braiders.

Orémi builds practical tools for the artists whose hands carry the culture. Our first product, Braid-EZ, helps automate hair braiding so braiders can work faster, protect their bodies, and grow on their own terms.

Braid-EZ helps automate the repetitive motion, while braiders stay in control of the style.
